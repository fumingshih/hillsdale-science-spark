Our Mission

Bringing the excitement of science directly to elementary classrooms

Educating & Inspiring the Next Generation

Science SPARK Club is a student-led initiative from Hillsdale High School dedicated to fostering scientific curiosity in elementary students through interactive, hands-on STEM activities.

Our passionate high school volunteers visit local elementary schools, bringing engaging experiments and demonstrations that make science accessible, fun, and memorable for young learners.

  • Interactive hands-on experiments designed for elementary students
  • Led by trained and enthusiastic high school volunteers
  • Activities covering physics, chemistry, biology, and engineering
  • Free resources for parents to recreate activities at home
